Contributing to our communities!


Did you happen to know? Every time you order our product, you're helping our local farmers and giving some funding

BE COOLA


If you join the COOLA club and become a special member: COOLA, you can be the first to get many chances
for our events, news, and promotion!



COOLA can get:


- BE FIRST: You can get all the news about our COOLA first.
- Beer Orders: in-store, by phone or online.
- Promotion: You can get a 5% discount coupon once a month.
- COOLA Club Event: If you have participated in the event at least once, you will get a 10% discount.
- Coupon: If you collect ten stamps, you get a discount of 3 dollars.
* You can get stamps if you buy anything in our store (both online and in-store.)
